Anita Borg Institute

The Anita Borg Institute is named after Anita Borg who worked tirelessly to promote and educate women in technology fields. Anita Borg founded the Grace Hopper Conference, now hosted by the Instittue, which works to promote and educate diversity in technology fields and has reached attendance of over 18,000 participants including students, industry professional and top industry executives.

Society of Women in Engineering

The Society of Women in Engineering is committed to bringing women and diversity into engineering. SWE provides a multitude of resources for their members including educational resources, scholarship, conferences and more!
